“Mockingbirds don't do one thing except make music for us to enjoy. They don't eat up people's gardens, don't nest in corn cribs, they don't do one thing but sing their hearts out for us. That's why it's a sin to kill a mockingbird.”

To kill a Mockingbird is Harper Lee's first novel and it was published in 1960. The setting is Maycomb, Alabama during the 1930's and it's based in her observations of her neighborhood. It has been very succesful in the United States becaoming a classic of modern American literature, many middle school and high school across the country. The book was banned because it was considered as immoral literature. it contains violence, offensive language and racism. The novel deals with the topic of rape and racism and some people may find it offensive.
